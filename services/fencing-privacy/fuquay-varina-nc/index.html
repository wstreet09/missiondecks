Fencing & Privacy Solutions in Fuquay-Varina FAQs

When should I install a fence on a new-construction FV lot?
Install your fence after the builder has completed final grading and the lot has settled for at least 30 days. Builder grading is often rough and can shift during the first few rainstorms, which would affect fence post alignment. If you are still within the builder's warranty period, verify that fence installation does not void any grading or drainage warranties. Most FV homeowners install fencing within the first 3–6 months of moving in.
What fence options are available in Bentwinds?
Bentwinds allows wood privacy fencing up to 6 feet in approved earth-toned stains. Vinyl is approved in select colors (tan, clay). The community prohibits painted fences, chain link, and metal post caps visible above the fence line. Golf course-adjacent lots may have additional restrictions to maintain sightlines. We design to Bentwinds standards and submit for architectural review with complete material specifications.
Is vinyl or wood fencing better for FV's newer neighborhoods?
For new-construction neighborhoods, vinyl has strong advantages: zero maintenance, no staining cycle, no rot or insect risk, and consistent appearance for 25–30 years. Wood (cedar or pressure-treated) is less expensive upfront but requires staining every 2–3 years and has a 15–20 year lifespan. In close-set FV lots where the fence is highly visible from both sides, vinyl's consistent appearance is often preferred. Cedar is the better choice if you want a natural, traditional aesthetic.
How fast can you install a privacy fence in Fuquay-Varina?
Most FV fence installations complete in 2–4 days. A standard 150-foot perimeter fence (three sides of a backyard) takes 3 working days: posts and concrete on day one, cure time, then rails and pickets over the following 1–2 days. We schedule FV projects efficiently due to our proximity from Holly Springs — drive time is under 15 minutes, so we can start early and maximize productive hours on site.
