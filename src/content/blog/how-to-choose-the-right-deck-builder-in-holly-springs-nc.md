## Check Credentials Before You Compare Prices

Credentials protect your investment. Price alone does not.

Before you choose a deck contractor, verify the basics:

-   **Business legitimacy:** Confirm the company is established and operating professionally.
-   **Insurance:** Ask for proof of general liability and workers’ compensation coverage.
-   **Permitting knowledge:** Ask who handles permit submissions and inspections in Holly Springs.
-   **References:** Request recent local projects, not just old or out-of-town examples.
-   **Written proposal:** Get a detailed scope of work, materials list, timeline, and payment schedule.

For permit-related guidance, homeowners can review building information through the [Town of Holly Springs official website](https://www.hollyspringsnc.gov). Requirements can change, so your contractor should be able to explain the current process clearly and specifically for Holly Springs, NC.

**Key Takeaway:** The best value is not the cheapest bid. It is the contractor who gives you a clear scope, uses quality materials, understands Holly Springs permitting, and stands behind the work.

## Questions to Ask Before You Hire

Smart questions reveal how a contractor works. Good answers are specific, not vague.

1.  **How many decks have you built in Holly Springs or nearby?**
2.  **Can you share recent local references?**
3.  **Who is my point of contact during construction?**
4.  **What is included in this proposal, line by line?**
5.  **How do you handle change orders?**
6.  **Will you manage permits and inspections?**
7.  **What warranty do you provide on workmanship?**

If a company gives rushed answers or avoids specifics, keep looking. Homeowners who want to choose a deck contractor wisely should expect transparency from the first meeting.
