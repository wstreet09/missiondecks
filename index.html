WHY MISSION DECKS

Where Design Meets Craftsmanship

We are a Holly Springs-based, owner-led outdoor living studio that handles both the creative design and the structural precision of every project. You work directly with the people who understand Triangle-area homes, neighborhoods, soil conditions, and building codes.

Every deck, porch, and outdoor kitchen we build starts with a conversation about how you actually use your space — not a template. We provide detailed, transparent proposals, realistic timelines with consistent communication, and craftsmanship that endures decades of North Carolina weather.
